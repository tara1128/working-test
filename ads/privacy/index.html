J. Opt out of seeing personalized ads

You can opt out of personalized ads in your Ads Settings. Here's what you can expect after you opt out:

You might see the same number of ads as before, but they might be less relevant because they aren't based on your interests.

You might still see ads related to the content in an application or based on other non-personal information.

1. Instructions for Android users

Ads Settings lets you opt out seeing personalized ads, please following the following instructions.

(1) Go to Ads Settings;

(2) Click or tap the slider next to Ads Personalization;

(3) Click or tap TURN OFF.

2. Instructions for iOS users

If you want to opt out of interest-based ads, you can choose to turn on the Limit Ad Tracking setting.

(1) Go to Settings ≷ Privacy ≷ Advertising;

(2) Turn on Limit Ad Tracking.
