APP LOCK

Tell your brand story with pre-social moments

App Lock is a popular security feature offered in Cheetah Mobile's utility apps, allowing users to protect their privacy by creating password-protected lock screens for individual apps. App Lock acts as the perfect "Pre-Social Moment" for advertisers to creatively reach social media app users.

This customizable ad format provides brands with the unique opportunity to reach social app users without advertising within the apps themselves. Three out of four users usually lock WhatsApp or Facebook app on their mobile phones.
